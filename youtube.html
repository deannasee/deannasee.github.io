Channel Optimization

While my current focus is on improving my video editing, here are some takeaways I had from many months of trial and error with channel optimization:

  1. Download a YouTube analytics extension. An extension like VidIQ can show you relevant tags and creators you fall in a similar niche as.
  2. Add core keywords to the description. Along those lines, write the description with the viewer in mind. Why should this video interest them? What can they gain from watching it?
  3. Design a cohesive brand image for your channel. The channel art, profile picture, and "About" tab should give the viewer and idea of who you are, what you post, and when to expect the next video.
