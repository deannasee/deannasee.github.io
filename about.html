About me
& my obsession
with making things

I am a Cognitive Science (Computer Science Concentration) and Media Studies double major, with a minor in Data Analytics at the University of Virginia.


Currently working as a Research Assistant at UVA Human Dynamics Lab.

My passion for digital creation stems from an intense desire to tell stories—a way to show how in an increasingly complex world, it’s the small things that bind us.

The smell of coffee in the morning. Twisting a jar open on your first try. Getting a text from a close friend you haven’t heard from in awhile. I grew up cherishing these moments, and was adamant to recreate this simplicity in digital spaces.

At UVA, I’ve spent most of my time exploring the intersection between my majors—and by extension, the fields of technology and media—to understand our relationship with digital spaces and how to hero the human in these experiences.


As a creator...

  • 🪛 I tinker with as many tools I can get my hands on—Adobe, Autodesk, Spark AR—to enhance digital experiences.
  • 🎧 I consider myself an interpreter, bridging conversations between different domains such as technology and business.
  • ❤️ I infuse my work with a personal touch—it should look, sound, and feel like something from another person.


As a person...

  • 🎶 Avid music lover/playlist curator, especially math rock and synthy music.
  • 🌎 Singaporean at heart but passionate about exploring new places.
  • 👩‍💻 Curious about new technologies and how they fit into our now/future.


Outside of work, I like to...

🎸 Play guitar, 📖 read, and 🖊️ write. 👟 Go on long walks to nowhere.
💃🏻 Dance like no one’s watching!
